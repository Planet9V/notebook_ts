# Product: Tetrel Notebook Operations Console

## Overview
Tetrel Notebook is an enterprise-grade AI-integrated data analytics and research platform. It supports four core user personas:
1.  **Sales Consultant**: Manages customer leads, pipeline, proposal generation, and compliance checks.
2.  **Researcher**: Performs deep research, document citation indexing, and semantic search.
3.  **Project Delivery Lead**: Monitors container telemetry, task lists, and SRE auto-healing recovery.
4.  **Marketing Manager**: Manages voice AI, podcast generation, and social schedulers.

## Core Feature Scopes
-   **Social Media Cron Tracker & Metrics**: Background cron tracking of reach, views, clicks, and interactions on scheduled posts, with a frontend analytics dashboard.
-   **Google Workspace Connectors**: Dynamic style guide compilation and SOW/report exporters for Google Docs, Sheets, and Slides.
-   **Observability & Logs**: Database-backed structured logging (Loguru + SurrealDB) and OpenTelemetry trace propagation across microservices.
